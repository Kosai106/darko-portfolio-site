Remington 1867 Navy Rolling Block Pistol

I'm a huge fan of 19th century weaponary. Here, I wanted to create a less common 19th century firearm where I replicate the case hardening (blue/brown colory stuff) on the metal while keeping in mind the process behind it. Triangle count is at around 3000 and it uses 1024x1024 texture sheets (diffuse, specular, normal & gloss). Rendered in Marmoset Toolbag 2.
