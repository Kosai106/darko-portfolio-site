Steyr Scout

This is a game-ready model I did for a project. I went for a relatively new look with the textures, focusing most on defining the materials to the best of my ability. Triangle count is at around 9000 and it uses 2048x2048 texture sheets (diffuse, specular, normal, gloss). Rendered in Marmoset Toolbag 2.
