Serbu Super Shorty Shotgun

This is a shotgun I made recently intended for game use. I wanted to make a quick and realistic presentation of this gun while making it visually interesting. Triangle count is at 2060 and it uses 1024x1024 texture sheets (diffuse, specular, normal and gloss maps). Rendered in Marmoset Toolbag 2.
