Overkill 2

Overkill 2 is a mobile game I worked on in 2012 with Craneballs Studio. I made a dozen of low resolution guns with attachments and all assets were later used in several other games (Overkill: Mafia, Overkill 3, Gun Master 3D, Gun Master 2 & Gun Master 3). All textures were done by various artists.
