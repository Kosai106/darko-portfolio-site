Overkill 2

Overkill 2 is a mobile game I worked on that was released a while back. I made a dozen of low resolution guns with attachments for it. The triangle limit was 6000 triangles for each gun with all attachments combined and all models are pushed towards that limit as per the project lead's instructions. At the bottom are models that never made it into the game as far as I know. All textures were done by various artists.
