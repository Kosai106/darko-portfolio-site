Devil's Third (PC/Wii U)

This is an old-gen game that I worked on in the first quarter of 2014 with Shapefarm. I was tasked with doing weapons for first person (and third person) use. The game was made in UE3 so the textures are old-gen and they use a grayscale specular map.
